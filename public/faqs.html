What's the difference between QKD and PQC?

QKD (Quantum Key Distribution) uses quantum principles to distribute keys securely, offering quantum information-theoretic security. PQC (Post-Quantum Cryptography) relies on purely classical algorithms designed to resist quantum attacks and is generally easier to deploy.
